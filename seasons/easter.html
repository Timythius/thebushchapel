Easter

Christ is risen! He is risen indeed! As the Australian autumn deepens and the mornings grow cool, we celebrate the greatest victory of all— death has been defeated, and new life has burst forth from the tomb. The Easter season extends for 50 days, giving us time to let the resurrection reality sink deep into our bones.
