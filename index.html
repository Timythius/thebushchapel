Welcome, Weary Traveller

Grab yourself a cuppa and join us in the quiet of the Australian bush. We gather to pray, reflect on scripture, sing around the piano and find rest for our souls.

Our gentle 15–20 minute services follow the Christian calendar with a printable liturgy to follow along with.

Our small voluntary team is made up of people from various denominations with qualifications and extensive experience in church ministry.

Whether you're joining us from the city or the country, you're welcome here, just as you are. We hope that you find comfort and blessing through the sacred seasons of faith.
