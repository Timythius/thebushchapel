Welcome, Weary Traveller

Grab yourself a cuppa and join us in the quiet of the Australian bush. Here you will find a simple chapel service made for those who need to worship from home or bedside.

We gather to pray, reflect on Scripture, sing around the piano and find rest for our souls.

Based on the lectionary, these gentle 15–20 minute services follow the same pattern each week with a printable transcript.

Our small voluntary team is made up of people from various denominations with qualifications and extensive experience in church ministry.

Whether you're joining us from the city or the country, you're welcome here, just as you are. We hope that you find comfort and blessing through the seasons of faith.
